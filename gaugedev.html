IN PROGRESS

I am currently developing a programmatically generated gauge in pure HTML-5. I am not even using PHP, but pure HTML-5, Jquery and Javascript.

Almost every team in a modern large company has monthly and yearly goals which are expressed in K.P.I. s Key Performance Indicatos A KPI is one number which expresses a key indicator of the team For example ASA - Average Speed of Answer shows which percentage of the calls have been answered correctly before the caller has been 30 seconds waiting on the line. For example 85% of the calls can be answered before 30 seconds have passed and on 15% of the rest of the calls the user had to wait longer In the companies there are a number of KPIs . Measuring KPIs is not difficult as in todays times the employees normally are working already on the computer. The idea is just to take those results and visualize them in realtime There is supposed to be a visually clearly differentiated gauge for each KPI Depending on the circumstances the scale can be adjusted

 

In computer games, there are 3D generated computer models which

in some way are more advanced, but they require a 3D engine of the game installed

 

The gauge which I am creating runs natively WITHOUT Flash in any browser without any plugins or separate installations. The user just needs his browser

 

Please use only Firefox for this time of development
